The bridge between Unity games and the DeSo (Decentralized Social) blockchain.

- **Purpose:** Enables game developers to easily integrate wallet login, NFT inventories, and social features (tips, posts) directly into their Unity projects.
- **Adoption:** Used by several projects in the DeSo ecosystem to bring blockchain mechanics to games.
- **Docs:** Extensive documentation and example scenes provided to jumpstart development.
