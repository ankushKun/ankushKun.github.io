A twist on the "Roles Reversed" theme.

**Premise:** Instead of playing the hero, you play as the entity controlling his luck.  
**Gameplay:** Manipulate dice rolls and probabilities in various minigames to help (or troll) the clumsy knight on his quest.  
Submitted to GMTK Game Jam 2023.
